About RS Gallery

Welcome to RS Gallery Art Gallery, your premier destination for a vivid showcase of artistic mastery. As an accomplished artist, 2D and 3D animator, I,Sandun Nayanaka, own this gallery which proudly presents a diverse portfolio of my creative works. At RS Gallery, you'll explore a mesmerizing collection that spans intricate 2D illustrations, dynamic 3D animations, and everything in between. Each piece is a testament to my passion for art and animation, capturing the essence of creativity and innovation. Step into my world, where art meets imagination, and witness the evolution of my craft. Whether you're here to admire, be inspired, or seek collaboration, RS Gallery Art Gallery is where my artistic journey unfolds before your eyes. Feel free to explore, and thank you for visiting my artistic haven. Enjoy the experience!

At RS Gallery, we specialize in a variety of art forms

  • Acrylic Painting: Explore vibrant and dynamic acrylic pieces that capture emotion and energy on canvas.
  • Portrait Painting: Discover lifelike and expressive portraits, meticulously crafted to bring out the essence of each subject.
  • Pencil Art: Enjoy the beauty of detailed pencil sketches and drawings, showcasing the intricacies and depth of monochromatic art.
  • Vector Drawing: Explore precise and scalable vector drawings, perfect for digital media and print, highlighting clean lines and vibrant graphics.
